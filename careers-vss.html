We look forward to bringing in likeminded dedicated people to grow with us in both BPO & Web development domains.

Part Time: Any 4 hour slot can be selected between 8AM and 7PM.

Rajendra Nagar, Dharapuram, Tamil Nadu

>> APIs working knowledge (PayPal, Google, Facebook, Payment gateways, etc.)

For Fresher: Starts from Rs.4000 per month.
